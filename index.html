MakerSpace

About

A Makerspace, is a collaborative and community-oriented workspace where people with a shared interest in making things can gather to create, invent, and learn. MakerSpaces provide access to various tools, equipment, and resources, typically including 3D printers, laser cutters, woodworking tools, electronics, sewing machines, and more. These spaces are designed to encourage hands-on learning, creativity, and innovation.

Our Vision

We think that all people should be able to make things without others being able to stop you in your tracks. Here you can think about anything and do anything with the volunteers, workers and host all on your side. Do things from building your own circuit to making your own robot!
